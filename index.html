The problem

Manual report generation is a time sink — every single Monday.

01

Every Monday = same Excel rebuild. You export data from ClientLook or RealNex, format it in Excel, add charts, and send it out. 2-3 hours per week. 100+ hours per year. Gone.

02

ClientLook/RealNex reporting is too basic. The CRM's built-in reports don't give you the format or metrics brokers actually want. You end up customizing in Excel anyway.

03

Manual reports have stale data by Wednesday. By the time brokers show them to principals, the numbers are outdated. Kills trust and credibility.

04

Principals want polished reports, not raw exports. They expect professional, branded PDFs. Excel dumps don't cut it.

How it works

Connect your CRM. Customize once. Sit back.

Step 1

Connect ClientLook or RealNex

PropReport reads your pipeline data directly from your CRM. No exports. No manual data entry. Setup takes under 5 minutes.

Step 2

Choose your report template

Customize metrics, branding, and layout once. Pick which fields to include, set your logo, and add your firm's colors. PropReport saves the template.

Step 3

Reports auto-generate every Monday

At 7am every Monday, PropReport pulls fresh data, generates a polished PDF, and emails it to your brokers and principals. Always on time. Always accurate.
